Colleges in Pune City

MIT-World Peace University

MIT-World Peace University (MIT-WPU) is renowned for its emphasis on peace and world-class education. It offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across diverse fields. Located in the heart of Pune, it provides a state-of-the-art campus and a global perspective to education.

College of Engineering Pune

The College of Engineering Pune (COEP) is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India. It offers a range of engineering and technical programs and is well-known for its research initiatives and industry collaborations.

Bharati Vidyapeeth

Bharati Vidyapeeth is a well-known educational institution offering a wide range of courses in various disciplines. It focuses on providing quality education and has a large network of campuses across India.
