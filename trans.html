Transportation in Pune

Transport in Pune includes:

Airways

Pune Airport (IATA: PNQ) is a major international airport located in Pune, Maharashtra. It serves as a crucial hub for domestic and international flights, providing connectivity and convenience for travelers to and from Pune.

Railways

Pune Junction (PUNE): Pune Junction is the main railway station serving the city. It is one of the busiest and most important railway stations in Maharashtra and serves as a key transit point for passengers traveling to various parts of India.

Metro

Pune Metro is a rapid transit system serving Pune, Maharashtra. It aims to reduce traffic congestion and enhance urban mobility. The network features modern trains and stations, providing efficient, eco-friendly transportation across the city.
